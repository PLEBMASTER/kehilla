A Family and Community Shul

With ongoing weekly and monthly programs for kids, the Westwood Kehilla is a great place for families. Our programs are for kids of all ages and include spiritual, educational and fun classes and events which leave everyone smiling!

Daily Minyanim

We are happy to have daily minyanim for Shacharit, Mincha and Maariv. Please check our schedule for this week’s Minyan times.

Fun For All Ages

We are a shul for the entire family. A community resource for great programming for people of all ages. We focus on bringing families together, and we provide meaningful opportunities for everyone to learn and grow in their Judaism at their own level.
